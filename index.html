FAQ

  • How do I submit a wait time?
    Select your location, enter hours and minutes, then click Submit.
  • How often can I submit?
    Once every 6 hours for either drive-thru or dine-in.
  • Why are there limits?
    Hours max 4, minutes max 59, and submissions must be between Business Hours of 10:30am-1:00am.
  • Is my data safe?
    Yes! No personal info is collected, this is free and community-run.
  • Can I submit just to test?
    Please don’t — it affects the displayed wait times.
