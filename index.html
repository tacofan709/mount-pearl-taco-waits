How does this work?

Community members submit the wait times they experience. All data is unofficial and for general guidance only.

What data is recorded?

Only the data you enter - time stamp, wait time, and if you selected drive-in or dine-in. We do not record any email addresses, phone numbers, IP addresses, etc.

Should I enter a wait time to just test the site?

No, please don't! This site is only as reliable as the data it receives. There is no need to "test the site" - I've done that ;)

I added my wait time, but the estimated wait time displayed didn't show exactly what I entered - does that mean this is broken?

No. This website shares the median estimated time from the last 10 answers provided over the last 90 minutes. Just because you enter a wait time of 15 minutes (for example) doesn't mean that the website will automatically dispaly an wait time of 15 minutes.

Why did you make this?

With all the excitement of Taco Bell's first standalone restaurant in NL, wait times have been very long (frequenly over 2 hours for the drive-thru) and I just thought this would be a fun and silly idea for people to share their wait times and maybe help others plan their visit to Taco Bell.

Who made this?

Hi! I'm Jeff Hillyard, a Mount Pearl resident who thought he was the most excited person for Taco Bell to come to town. Turns out I'm not the only one! I work for a local nonprofit as a program coordinator. Wed Dev is not my dayjob.

Are you making any money from this app?

No. I'm not sure if there is even a way to monitize this, but I have no interest in making money from this tool.
